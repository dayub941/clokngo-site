Who We Are

At CloknGo, we're a new business with a big mission: To make timekeeping simple, accurate, and affordable for small businesses.

Why CloknGo?

We offer enterprise-grade features at a fraction of the cost. Whether you're a startup, a growing business, or a freelancer, CloknGo is built to scale with your timekeeping needs.

What We Offer

  • Simple Time Tracking: Easy clock-in/out tools from any device
  • Reports: Exportable timesheets for payroll and productivity
  • Affordable Pricing: Transparent monthly plans with no hidden fees
  • Cloud-Based: Accessible anywhere, anytime
  • Secure & Reliable: Your data is encrypted and always safe

A Note From Our Founder

My name is Diana, and I’m proud to be the President & CEO of CloknGo. With over 15 years of experience supporting public organizations through voluntary benefits and payroll systems, I saw firsthand how small businesses often struggle with timekeeping solutions that are either too expensive or too complex. I started CloknGo to change that.

My goal is simple: to give hardworking business owners a tool that’s reliable, easy to use, and cost-effective. Thank you for trusting us — we’re here to support you every step of the way.
